## Outreach Strategy

**Timing:** Do not start outreach until TASK-031 is complete (Frontend QMS CAPA screen).
You cannot demo the platform without a working UI. The screen is the hook.

**Approach:**
1. Connect on LinkedIn first (no cold email as first contact — too easy to ignore)
2. Send connection note referencing a specific public signal (CQO appointment, facility acquisition, job posting)
3. After connection accepted: 3-sentence message. Pain → solution → ask for 20 minutes
4. Follow up once after 7 days. No more than 2 touches per contact before moving to next

**What to offer:**
- Platform access free for 12 months
- In exchange: 4 structured feedback sessions (once a month), case study rights after 6 months, right to reference the deployment in fundraising materials
- Make it explicit: "You are shaping the product — your QA workflows become the template"

**What NOT to do:**
- Do not offer a pitch deck as the first interaction
- Do not lead with the technology; lead with their specific pain
- Do not send a demo video without a live conversation first
